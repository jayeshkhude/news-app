How it works
NewsLens collects articles from six major Indian and international outlets every four hours.
Articles on the same topic are grouped automatically; each bundle is tagged with a rough category for variety on the homepage.
An AI compares what the sources actually report (not just matching words) and writes a short plain-language summary.
Every four hours the feed is re-ranked for importance; source links stay tied to that summary only.
